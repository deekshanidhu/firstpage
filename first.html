The School of Management Sciences (SMS) is a private autonomous college in Varanasi that was established in 1995. It is affiliated with AKTU and MGKVP and is ranked among the top 50 B-schools in India. It is accredited by NAAC as an 'A' grade institution. SMS Varanasi is considered one of the most sought-after colleges in the country for pursuing management, computer sciences, commerce, and mass communication programs. It is known for offering the best employment-oriented education, a holistic academic environment, and amazing placement opportunities in Purvanchal and even the entire Uttar Pradesh.
